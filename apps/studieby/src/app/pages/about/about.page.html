About

Tour de Chambre is a vibrant one-day program designed to welcome new students to Copenhagen by inviting them to explore their new home.

The program includes free access to various cultural venues, culminating in a gathering at Enghave Park featuring music, talks, and a range of events. As the evening goes on, the festivities continue with special offerings at some of the city’s bars and nightclubs.

Tour de Chambre is organised by NusNus and Københavns Kommune as part of Studieby.

Studieby is an initiative by Københavns Kommune providing insights and resources for students in Copenhagen.
For more information, visit Studieby.kk.dk

NusNus, the organisation behind Distortion festival, Karrusel festival, the club Hangaren, and a creative agency, brings its event-making expertise to Tour de Chambre.
