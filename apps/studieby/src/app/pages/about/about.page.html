About

Tour de Chambre is a one-day program designed to welcome new students to Copenhagen by inviting them to explore their new home. This initiative, in collaboration with some of the country’s largest cultural institutions, student organisations, and nightlife partners, offers a day filled with diverse activities.

The program includes free access to various cultural venues, followed by a gathering at Enghave Park featuring music, talks, and various events. The day concludes with special offerings at key nightlife institutions.

Tour de Chambre is organised by NusNus and Københavns Kommune as part of Studieby.

Studieby is an initiative by Københavns Kommune providing insights and resources for students in Copenhagen.
Studieby.kk.dk

NusNus, the organisation behind Distortion festival, Karrusel festival, the club Hangaren, and a creative agency, brings its event-making expertise to Tour de Chambre.
